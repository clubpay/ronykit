Every service module follows a fixed service.go boilerplate. The package name is the service name suffixed with "mod" (e.g. authmod, ledgermod, notifmod). The Service struct holds *api.Service and *fx.App. Expose App(opt ...fx.Option) *Service to build the service via fx.New(appModule, fx.Populate(&apiSvc), fx.Options(opt...)). Expose Module(opt ...fx.Option) fx.Option that wraps App() in an fx.Provide with fx.Lifecycle hooks for Start/Shutdown. Register the service in init() via di.RegisterService[Service] with Kind ("service" or "gateway"), Name (derived from settings.ModuleName), InitFn (LoadSettings), and ModuleFn (Module). Delegate Desc() to svc.api.Desc(). Implement Start(ctx) and Shutdown(ctx) by forwarding to svc.fxApp.Start/Stop.
